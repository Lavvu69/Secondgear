Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about buying, selling, payments, services, and account support on SecondGear.

Buying Cars

How do I buy a car on SecondGear?

Go to the Buy page, choose a listed car, open details, and continue to purchase. Fill your checkout details and submit payment to complete the order.

Can I negotiate the price?

Yes. Use the Make Offer option on the Buy page. Admin can accept, reject, or counter your offer.

What happens when my offer is accepted?

You receive a notification with a checkout link. Open it and complete purchase using the agreed offer price.

How do I know if a car is still available?

Only available cars appear in active listings. Once purchased, the car is marked sold and removed from available stock.

Selling Cars

How can I list my car for sale?

Go to the Sell page and submit car details and photos. Admin reviews your request and approves valid listings.

How long does approval take?

Approval time depends on review queue and data completeness. Listings with clear images and complete information are processed faster.

Can I track my submitted listing?

Yes. You can track listing status from your profile/listings area after login.

Payments & Pricing

Which payment methods are supported?

Current checkout options include card payments, PayPal, and cash on delivery, depending on the flow shown at purchase.

Will I receive a purchase confirmation?

Yes. Purchase confirmation is stored in your account history and invoice communication is sent to the registered email when available.

Is offer-agreed price saved in records?

Yes. Final deal price is stored for purchase and sold-car records, including agreed offer/counter-offer transactions.

Service Booking

How do I book a vehicle service?

Use the Service page, choose service type, date, and time slot, then submit your booking request.

Can I cancel or reschedule service?

Yes. In your profile, open bookings and use cancel or reschedule actions where available.

Account & Security

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Use the forgot-password flow in user login. Reset requests are reviewed through admin verification for security.

How do I update my profile details?

Log in and open My Profile to update your name, phone, and password.

Where can I see notifications?

Open the Messages section in your profile to view offer updates, approvals, and other account notices.
